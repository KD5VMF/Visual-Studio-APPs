PFLT13 Rev 13 - pfSense Live Telemetry

Rev 13 fixes the bad Mbps/Gbps number problem across the whole dashboard.

What changed:
- Raw SNMP byte counters stay raw internally.
- Rates are calculated once from byte deltas per second.
- Mbps/Gbps display auto-scales cleanly:
  - below 1000 Mbps = Mbps
  - 1000 Mbps and above = Gbps
- Counter resets and interface reselects are detected instead of becoming fake giant Gbps values.
- 32-bit SNMP counter rollover is handled correctly.
- Impossible one-sample spikes are filtered so Max Download, Max Upload, Total Now, Top Ports, and graphs do not get poisoned.
- Top Upload/Download port cards use the same safe scaling/filtering.
- App/settings/log folder changed to Documents\PFLT13.

Run:
1. Open the PFLT13 folder.
2. Double-click build_and_run.cmd.

If Windows blocks the app, right-click the ZIP, Properties, Unblock, then extract again.
